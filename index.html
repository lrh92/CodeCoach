What we do

Where next?

Many websites and resources exist to help people teach themselves how to code, but who do you turn to when you get stuck - when you complete the HTML track on CodeCademy and don't know what to do next? We know what to do.

Learning

We help match mentors with learners who can give tips and pointers on where to start with coding and how to progress through the basics and beyond. After you sign up, you'll choose whether you want to be a mentor or a mentee.

View details »

Making Connections

CodeCoach members can choose from a number of different ways of getting in contact with each other - messaging, forums or video chat. You can create your own unique profile with links to your GitHub repository and other project work.

Mentees

If you're hoping to improve your coding skills and are getting stuck on something you can't find help with anywhere, set up a request and a mentor will come to the rescue. Many build great relationships with their mentors - we've even had mentees go on to become mentors themselves!

Mentors

Mentors are the best thing about our project - they give up their time to help budding coders find their way!
